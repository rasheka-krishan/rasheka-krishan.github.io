Foggy Descent

Joker Inspired Book

Everything about this book was about the character Joker from the Batman comics. The colors, the inkblots, the quotes. Through my research I realized how much psychology was involved in his character which I portrayed through the inkblots as a fun way for people to interact with this book.

Sci-Fi Manifesto

For this project I had to create a little book out of a manifesto of my choice. This manifesto is quite funny to me because it talks about how sci-fi is being turned into more fiction than science and the writter is angry. I decided I wanted to play with layout for this project which is why the paper is so long. The way the manifesto was written was very formal and official sounding which made me think of this bigger and more porminent layout.

Font Accordian

This is an assignment where I created my own font and made a little promotional package to send to people who may be interested in using it. This assignment was very handsy as I cut the fonts out without any lines to follow and with scissors. The letters came out very interesting because there weren't any guidelines except for looking at images of old alphabets. In the end I created a cohesive system and a matching envelope. The book takes an accordian fold form.

Water on Mars

This is a wikipedia page turned into a book. The subject matter allowed me to use different typefaces like typewritter fonts for headings because it reminds me of space. The big creative decision I made this book was having the cover and the start of chapeter all black with one side of the text white and the other side gradiented, as if it's reflective, like water. This book allowed me to work with long pieces and learn how to find balances between multiple typefaces.

Emotional Type

This was a website I made inspired by a typography assignment. The project was to choose an onomatopoeia word and convey certain emotions with them. I translated that book I created into a website. If you click on the emoji's the text animates and you're able to enter and change the words at anytime.

Collection Website

Location Specific Design

This website was specifically designed for the phone. It doesn't work on the desktop because it's supposed to be used outside. It's a performance based website where a group of people are gathered and follow the instructions they receive. Music is played in the background and the interface is a casette tape to give the ambiance of a performace. My attempt was to get a group of people to spread out in an area and to see where they land and what they see alone the way.
